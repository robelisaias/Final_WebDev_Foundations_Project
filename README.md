## 📦 Project Overview

This project is a **dynamic web application** that serves as an online portal for African and imported goods. It is designed to help users easily browse, search, and discover culturally relevant products that are often difficult to find in mainstream retail stores.

The application focuses on delivering a clean, responsive, and interactive experience while showcasing items such as food products, spices, clothing, and everyday essentials commonly used in African households.

---

## 👤 User Persona

**Name:** Selam Tesfay  
**Age:** 32  
**Location:** Toronto, Canada  
**Background:** Originally from Eritrea, living abroad for several years  

Selam often looks for familiar products from home—such as traditional spices, snacks, and household items—but finds it difficult to locate them in nearby stores. She values convenience and prefers an online platform where she can quickly search for products and discover alternatives.

### 🎯 Goals
- Easily find authentic African/imported goods  
- Save time by avoiding multiple store visits  
- Discover new or similar products  

### 😕 Frustrations
- Limited availability in local supermarkets  
- Poorly designed or outdated online stores  
- Lack of filtering and clear product details  

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Many individuals living outside of Africa face challenges when trying to find authentic African and imported goods. Existing solutions are often inconsistent, difficult to navigate, or lack essential features like search and filtering.

This creates a frustrating and time-consuming shopping experience.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

This project provides a **centralized and user-friendly platform** where users can:

- Browse a curated collection of products  
- Search and filter items in real time  
- View product details in an organized layout  
- Access a live widget powered by a public API  
- Interact with a functional and validated form  

---

## 🧱 Features

- 🛍️ **Dynamic Product Gallery** (JavaScript-based)

## ⚠️ Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge:**  
Implementing real-time filtering for the product gallery while keeping the UI responsive.

**Solution:**  
Used JavaScript event listeners on the search input and dynamically updated the DOM by filtering a local data array, ensuring fast and smooth user interaction without page reloads.
